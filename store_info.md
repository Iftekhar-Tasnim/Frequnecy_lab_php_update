# Store Category Plan (Mega Menu Ready)

This document defines a **clean, scalable category structure** for an electronics & robotics store. It is optimized for **mega menu navigation**, filtering, and future inventory expansion.

---

## Top-Level Menu Structure (Recommended)

1. **Robotics & Kits**
2. **Sensors & IoT**
3. **Components & Displays**
4. **Power & Tools**
5. **Industrial Solutions** (Optional)

---
